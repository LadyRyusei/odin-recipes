Beans N' Toast

Beans on toast is a simple and satisfying dish that is a staple in many households. The combination of warm, baked beans with the crisp and crunchy texture of toasted bread creates a comforting and wholesome meal. The aroma of buttery toast mixed with the rich, savory smell of baked beans makes your mouth water with anticipation. Each bite is a perfect balance of savory and slightly sweet flavors, with the creaminess of the beans complementing the crunchiness of the toast.

This recipe is easy to customize by adding your own toppings or spices to suit your taste preferences. With the option to add your own toppings, such as melted cheese, avocado, or hot sauce, this classic dish is versatile and always delicious. It's a quick and filling meal that's perfect for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. It's no wonder that beans on toast is a beloved comfort food around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of baked beans
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste (Be wary of pepper! It's super spicy!)
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheddar cheese, sliced avocado, chopped tomatoes, hot sauce

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
  2. While the bread is toasting, heat the baked beans in a small saucepan over medium heat. Stir occasionally until heated through.
  3. Once the toast is toasty, spread butter on each slice.
  4. Spoon the hot baked beans on top of the toast.
  5. Season with salt and pepper (not too much!!!) to taste.
  6. Add any optional toppings as desired.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
