Beans N' Toast

British baked beans are a delicious and hearty dish that is a staple in many British breakfasts. These baked beans are made with haricot beans, a type of small white bean, that are simmered in a rich tomato sauce with a blend of spices and seasonings. The sauce has a slightly sweet and tangy flavor, thanks to the addition of molasses, vinegar, and a hint of Worcestershire sauce. The beans themselves are tender and creamy, with a satisfying bite that pairs perfectly with the bold and flavorful sauce. British baked beans are typically served on top of buttered toast, making for a filling and satisfying breakfast. They are also a popular accompaniment to traditional English breakfasts, alongside bacon, eggs, and black pudding; but all of those pale in comparison to beans on toast. Overall, British baked beans are a comforting and delicious dish that is sure to warm you up on a chilly morning or satisfy your hunger any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of haricot beans (or other white beans), drained and rinsed
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes (14 oz)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon of molasses
  • 1 tablespoon of white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • Salt and black pepper (very spicy!!! BE CAREFUL), to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic and saut√© for 2-3 minutes, until softened.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, molasses, white vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper to the saucepan. Stir well to combine. Go light on the spices if they hurt your tummy.
  3. Add the drained and rinsed haricot beans to the saucepan and stir gently to coat them in the sauce.
  4. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and let it cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened and the beans are tender.
  5. Taste the beans.
  6. Serve the baked beans hot on top of buttered toast, and ONLY buttered toast.
